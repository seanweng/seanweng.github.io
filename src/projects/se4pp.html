AI Strategy Competition Platform

Web Application for Programming Education

About the Project

This project aims to motivate the students in the Introduction to Computers course for self-directed learning in programming. The AI Strategy Competition Platform provides an online competition platform for board games. Students can construct their AI strategy using C or Java to compete with each other on the platform. The platform also provides a ranking system to encourage students to obtain higher rankings by improving AI strategies. In 2020, to meet the new requirements, a 6 people team was built to migrate inapplicable frameworks from Python2 to Python3. With the evolved platform, over 70% of the students actively hone their programming skills by improving their AI strategies during the 2021 spring semester. The success of the platform earned all project members an Excellent Teaching Assisting Award in 2021.
